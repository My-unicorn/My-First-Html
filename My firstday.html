    How to make cupcakes step by step

    1. Cream together 1/3 cup of butter with 1/2 cup of sugar until light and moist.
    2. Then add 1 tsp of Vanila extract and mix it in.
    3. Add 2 eggs (one at the time) to the batter.
    4. Then add 1 1/4 cups of all purouse flour+ 1 1/4 tsp of baking soda + 1/4 cup of milk. Mix well untill all its of flour disappear.
    5. Preheat oven to 350 fahrenheit
    6. Line your cupcake pan with cupcake liners
    7. Add the batter into to the capcake liners.
    8. Bake the cupcake for 15-20 minutes.
    9. Let them cool for a few minutes in the pan then take them out.
    10. Voila! Your awesome and delicios cupcakes are done.
